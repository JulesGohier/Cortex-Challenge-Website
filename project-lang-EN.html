What is the cortex challenge?

First of all, before being a computer science project, it is necessary to know that the cortex challenge is a board game whose principle is to solve different challenges as quickly as possible. It has eight different tests that work on different forms of intelligence such as memory, logic, observation and coordination. For each form of intelligence, you have to collect two cards to win a piece of brain and the goal is to get the four different pieces of brain to win.

Our project is based on the principle of the board game, with the difference that we have to solve the eight challenges through programming, in Python language. We have to propose at least two types of algorithms that allow us to solve each of the proposed challenges. The goal is to solve them as quickly as possible - while giving a maximum of good answers. In the end, a competition will take place between each group to make the comparison.

The eight proposed challenges are : Color, Reflection, Calculation, Frequency, Missing, Labyrinth, Duplicate and Reasoning. Each one is represented as a json file, containing several information such as the name of the challenge or its data to solve it.
